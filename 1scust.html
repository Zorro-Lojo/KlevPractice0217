KLEV S-Custom

KLEV S-Custom Line guitars are custom-made Fender Stratocaster replicas, built to the highest possible quality.
Each S-Custom guitar is 100% made to order, starting from KLEV's signature paint job, all the way to the very bolts used to assemble the guitar.

Imagination is the only limit to what a S-Custom guitar can be. Crafted to highly exact standards using cutting-edge industrial processes, KLEV S-Custom guitars guarantee clean, pitch perfect playing and amazing note sustain matching the best limited-series guitars, for a fraction of the price. For the discerning guitar player who wants something truly unique both in terms of looks and sound, there is nothing like the S-Custom.
